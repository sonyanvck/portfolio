Glaucoma

Different types of glaucoma include POAG, ACG, and many more.

Management of glaucoma includes prescription drops and in severe cases, surgery.

Drops include:

  • Latanoprost
  • Brimonidine
  • Dorzolamide

Dry Eye Symptoms* include:

  1. Burning sensation
  2. Excessive tearing
  3. Fluctuating vision

*This is not a conclusive list of all symptoms.
